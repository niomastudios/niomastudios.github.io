Hi there!

I am a designer, lecturer and design-consultant based in Machakos, Kenya with a deep knowledge in application of design elements and principles. I received a B.A in Clothing, Textiles and Interior Design at Egerton University and a Master in Design from the University of Nairobi. The practical and academic experience I have gathered has given me proven ability in design research and handling design projects.
I am also a strong planner and enthusiastic problem solver who readily adapts to change, works with zero supervision while exceeding expectations. I meet design project deadlines without compromising on quality.
I am particularly good in various multi-media softwares thus can deliver excellent design presentations.
I am an ambivert with good interpersonal communication exposure. My strong background in art and design gives me a creative outlook on how I work around challenges.

Interior Design
